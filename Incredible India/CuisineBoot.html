Popular Indian Dishes

Expolre the flavors of India through its diverse and rich culinary traditions!

North Indian Cuisine

North India is known for its rich, aromatic and flavourful dishes often cooked with ghee and spices:

  • Biryani: A fragrant rice dish made with aromatic spices and meat vegetables.
  • MalaiKofta: A traditional North Indian dish consisting of fried potato and paneer balls in a creamy sauce.
  • PaneerTikka: Marinated paneer cubes grilled to perfection.

Aromatic and flavourful Biryani.


South Indian Cuisine

South India offers a wide array of dishes with a perfect blend of spices and tangy flavours:

  • Masala Dosa: A crispy rice crepe stuffed with spicy potato filling.
  • Idli and Sambar: Soft rice cakes served with a tangy lentil-based vegetable stew.li>
  • Capsicum Curry: A fiery, flavourful dish from Kerala.

South Indian favourite: Masala Dosa


Sweet Delights

No Indian meal is complete without indulging in some mouth-watering desserts:

  • Gulab Jamun: Fried milk solids soaked in sugar syrup.
  • Rasgulla: Soft and spongy cheese balls in light sugar syrup.
  • Payasam: A South Indian dessert made of milk, rice and jaggery.
